About Us

We are passionate gamers who love playing video games and sharing our accomplishments with other gamers. One day one of our friends noticed that there were no places where we could search for any data specific to our account so we came up with an idea. We want to create a platform where we can search for specific data on the games we play. Game Kit allows us to do that, with the help of various Steam APIs we successfully create a site where gamers can look for their data, as well as data from their friends or any public accounts. We hope that Game Kit can be a useful tool for gamers and help grow the community of people who love playing video games whether it be casual or competitive players.
